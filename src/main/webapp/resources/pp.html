Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following statement will help you understand how our app deals with the personal information you may occasionally provide to us via the Internet

Generally, we do not collect any personal information from you when you download our applications. To be specific, we do not require the consumers to be registered before downloading the application, nor do we keep track of the consumers' visits of our application. The only situation we may get access to your personal information is when you personally decide to email us at the end of the programme, provide your feedback or to provide us with a bug report. The information we may get from you in these situations are strictly limited to your name, email address and your response.

In the above situations, we guarantee that your personal information will only be used for contacting you and improving our services. We will never use such information (e.g. your name and email address) for any other purposes, such as to further market our products or to disclose your personal information to a third party for commercial gains.

It should be noted that whether you contact us or not is a completely voluntary decision. If you have concern about your personal information being misused or if you want further information about our privacy policy and what it means, please feel free to email us at enquiries@chityogsadhana.com and we will endeavor to provide clear answers to your questions in a timely manner.
